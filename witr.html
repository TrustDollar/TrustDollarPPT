Withdrawal Information

Minimum Withdrawal

You can withdraw a minimum of $20. Make sure your wallet has enough balance.

Maximum Withdrawal

The maximum withdrawal per transaction is $40,000 to ensure safety.

How to Withdraw

Select your preferred method, enter the amount, and click the "Request Withdrawal" button. Ensure all details are correct to avoid failed transactions.

Wallet Verification

Before making a full withdrawal, please first withdraw a small amount between $21 - $22. This ensures your wallet address is safely verified with us, making it easier for us to send your funds quickly and securely.

Note: For new IDs, these rules are mandatory to verify your wallet before performing any larger withdrawals. Whenever you add a new wallet address, please first make a withdrawal of $21 or $22.

Withdrawal Guidelines

Withdrawals are processed within max.. 2 to 8 hours. Double-check your wallet address before confirming.
